Spa Etiquette & Policy

  • Booking in advance is **highly recommended**.
  • The **minimum treatment value** per Spa Therapist is **R800**.
  • Please advise us of any health conditions, allergies, injuries, or recent medical/cosmetic procedures.
  • To confirm your booking, **50% of the treatment payment is required**.
  • Please be ready **15 minutes** prior to your booking time.
  • A **call out fee** may be added depending on your location.

Cancellation Policy:

24-hour advance notice is required for cancellation and rescheduling. If this is not adhered to, **50% of the treatment will be charged**. Late arrivals will result in a reduction of treatment time whilst the full treatment fee will apply.
